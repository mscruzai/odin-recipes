Espresso Martini Cocktail

Description

This espresso martini is a sweet and delicious after-dinner drink made with espresso, coffee liqueur, vodka, and white creme de cacao for a coffee lovers dream cocktail.

A vodka martini is a vodka cocktail made with espresso and coffee liqueur. The drink originated in the 1980s and experienced a surge in popularity in the 2020s. The act of shaking the espresso with the ice, liquor, and liqueurs creates a creamy, foamy topping.

Espresso martinis are traditionally served chilled in martini glasses. For a fancy touch, rim the glass with cinnamon-sugar and garnish with coffee beans.

Ingridients

  • 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua)
  • 1 fluid ounce white creme de cacao
  • 1 fluid ounce brewed espresso
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour vodka, coffee liqueur, crème de cacao, and espresso into a cocktail shaker; add ice, cover and shake until chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
